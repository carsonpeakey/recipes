Quick Pho

October 19, 2024
Modified November 3, 2024

Ingredients

  • 64 oz broth (beef, chicken, veg)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 star anise
  • ginger
  • 1/2 onion
  • bok choy
  • protein (beef, chicken, tofu, meatballs)
  • noodles (udon, vermicelli, etc)

Garnish

  • 2t fish sauce
  • bean sprout
  • green onions
  • jalapenos

Equipment

  • cutting board
  • sharp knife
  • 4 qt pot (or 2 to speed things up)

Prep

  • peel the ginger and cut it in half
  • cook the noodles (this can be done while the broth is simmering, if you are using 2 pots)
  • chop the veg

Cook

  • simmer the broth for at least 30min
  • add noodles to bowls
  • ladle hot broth into bowls
  • garnish (or provide self-serve garnish)
  • serve
