Oatmeal

October 19, 2024
Modified November 9, 2024

Equipment

  • Instant Pot
  • Scoopy spatula

Ingredients

These quantities produce 2 servings

  • 1/2 C oat mix (steel cut, rolled)
  • 1 1/4 C Liquid
    • 1/2 C Water
    • 3/4 C Soy Milk

Cook

Pressure cook 4 minutes (natural venting), rest 12.

Serve

Portion into bowls, top with Super jam and nut butter
