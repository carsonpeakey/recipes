Hummus

Equipment 1/4 C measure Citrus juicer (optional, just squeeze with your hands) Food processor Knife Tablespoon Ingredients 2/3 C dry chickpeas (soaked overnight and pressure-cooked 10 minutes) 2-3 Lemons 2 garlic cloves Salt Tahini Cumin Paprika Olive oil Prep Clean and chop the garlic Halve and juice the lemons Prepare the food processor with its chopping blade Cook Add the garlic, oil, cumin, and lemon juice Pulse to break up the garlic, then run continuously for 20-30 seconds to homegenize the mixture
