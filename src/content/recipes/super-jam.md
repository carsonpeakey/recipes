:::equipment
- Medium sauce pan
- Spatula
- Closable container for storage (if you're making a lot)
:::

:::ingredients
- Frozen raspberries
- Frozen blueberries
- Chia seeds
- Neutral oil
:::

:::cook

#### Get started

1. Begin heating the sauce pan on low
1. Add some oil to the pan
1. Add the frozen fruit

#### Tend

1. Stir every few minutes until the fruit has thawed into a thick liquid

:::callout
You don't want any more than the barest of simmers. If the simmer becomes a
boil, lower the heat!
:::

#### Finish

1. Simmer down to the consistency you want
1. When it's close, add 1T Chia seeds for each cup of fruit in your pan

:::

:::serve
1. Spoon onto toast, waffles, or oatmeal
1. Save for later in the fridge in a sealable container
:::
