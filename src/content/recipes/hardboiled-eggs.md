::::ingredients
- Dozen eggs
- Water (1C)
::::

::::equipment
Instant Pot + trivet
::::

::::prep
1. Place trivet in the bottom of the instant pot
1. Add 1 C water
1. Add eggs to the trivet (carefully, one at a time)
::::

::::cook
1. Set Instant Pot
   - Pressure cook
   - 6 minutes
   - Natural venting

1. Prepare your preferred cool-down method:
   - Ice bath
   - Seal-able container

1. Once pressure cooking time has elapsed, wait 3 minutes
1. Release pressure
1. Cool down the eggs using one of the following methods
   1. Ice Bath
      - Transfer eggs to the ice bath
      - Once cool, transfer eggs to a clean kitchen towel on the counter to air-dry
   1. Fridge
      - Transfer the eggs to a seal-able container
      - Seal it and put the container in the fridge
::::
