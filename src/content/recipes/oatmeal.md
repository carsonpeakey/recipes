::::equipment
- Instant Pot
- Scoopy spatula
::::

::::ingredients

:::callout
These quantities produce 2 servings
:::

- 1/2 C oat mix (steel cut, rolled)
- 1 1/4 C Liquid
  - 1/2 C Water
  - 3/4 C Soy Milk

::::

::::cook

Pressure cook 4 minutes (natural venting), rest 12.

::::

::::serve

Portion into bowls, top with [Super jam](/recipes/super-jam/) and nut butter
